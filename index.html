1. Identify the Lead
with Big Data

We create a highly targeted prospect list of pre-approved consumers by combining information from multiple databases.  We use a fifty-point attribute profile, including:  credit scores, payment behavior, income levels, monthly expenditures, property valuations (lender-grade AVM), neighborhood statistics, demographic data and lifestyle characteristics.

These data points are then used to create a Response Score (likelihood to respond) using statistical regression techniques. We only solicit the top scoring prospects.

2. Contact the Lead
with Direct Mail

Using the data from our fifty-point profile we create deeply personalized direct mail campaigns. We use the latest ink-jet web printing technologies to create stunning mail pieces with customized envelopes. Our patent-pending MortgageMapTM technology allows us to print a high resolution satellite photo of the clients home that is overlaid with the property descriptions and the most recent home valuation (AVM).

These mailers are then supported by direct e-mail, re-targeted banner ads, and social media campaigns.

3. Convert the Lead with
Frictionless Technologies

We convert the lead to a credit application using a TurboTax-like online wizard that is truly frictionless. How do we do it? Simple, we use a pre-populated credit application changing the client experience from “typing” a credit app to simply “verifying” the information we’ve already collected. The client just points and clicks – and that’s what makes it so effortless to complete.

Once verified, the application and contact data is instantly transferred into your CRM via a secured data API.
